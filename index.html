Sports

I worked for over ten years for Kansas Athletics, Inc. in the video department. The video department is responsible for creation of the content and running of all the electronic signage in Allen Fieldhouse, Memorial Stadium, Hoglund Ballpark and Rock Chalk Park. I will forever be a Jayhawk. Rock Chalk!

Graphic Design & Sublimation

I became proficient in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator while working at Kansas Athletics. After becoming bored with my Cricut in 2019, I discoverd the world of sublimation. Sublimation is a process that turns ink from a liquid into a solid with heat and bonds permanently to the substrate. The possiblities are endless. However, substrates have to be white or of a light color and if it is fabric must contain at least 65% polyester.
